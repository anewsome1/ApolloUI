--- layout: component title: Card sizing category: card description: Like most other components, cards are fluid-width by default. The recommended way to size cards is by putting them inside grid columns, which gives you control over their size at each breakpoint and includes gutters between the cards. ---

Because cards are fluid-width, any sort of content can be put inside a card. Just keep in mind that fluid images will scale to fit and may get very small, depending on how much space you have allocated for the card.
