--- title: Card deck category: card order: 6 description: Card decks are handy for making cards align horizontally and match both width and height. The .card-body is designed to fill in the available space, so .card-footer appears at the bottom of the card (when flexbox mode is enabled).

Note that the .card-deck-wrapper class is only necessary if you have flexbox mode disabled. ---

Because cards are fluid-width, any sort of content can be put inside a card. Just keep in mind that fluid images will scale to fit and may get very small, depending on how much space you have allocated for the card.
